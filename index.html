Our Philosophy

A New Standard of Care

Selora Aesthetics is a med spa in Woodbridge, Ontario, delivering evidence-based treatments with modern technology and personalized care plans designed for natural-looking, confidence-boosting results. We specialize in professional skincare and advanced aesthetic services, including customized facials (detox, acne, anti-aging), chemical peels, dermaplaning, microneedling, microdermabrasion, hydro facials, and laser hair removal sessions and packages. To complete the experience, we also offer relaxing add-ons like lymphatic face massage and head massage in a safe, professional setting.

Booking Policies

Booking Appointments

  • All appointments must be scheduled in advance.
  • A deposit may be required to secure your booking (non-refundable, but transferable if rescheduled within the policy timeframe).
  • Same-day appointments are based on availability.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Please provide 24–48 hours' notice if you need to reschedule or cancel.
  • Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice, or no-shows, will result in a forfeited deposit.
  • Arrivals later than 15 minutes may require rescheduling.

Payments

  • Payments are accepted via cash or e-transfer at this time.
  • All deposits are applied toward your treatment cost.
  • Packages and memberships are non-refundable but may be transferred to another service.

Health & Safety

  • Please arrive with clean skin (no makeup on the treatment area).
  • Notify me of any medical conditions, medications, or recent procedures before your appointment.
  • If you are sick or feeling unwell, please reschedule.

Results & Aftercare

  • Results vary per individual, and consistency is key.
  • Aftercare instructions will be provided — please follow them carefully.
  • Selora Aesthetics is not liable for results compromised due to not following pre- and post-care guidelines.

My goal is to provide a relaxing, professional, and welcoming environment.

Kindly respect these policies so that I can continue to give every client the best possible care.
